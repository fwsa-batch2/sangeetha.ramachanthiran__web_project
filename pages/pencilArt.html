Pencil Drawing


     Pencil drawing, drawing executed with an instrument composed of graphite enclosed in a wood casing and intended either as a sketch for a more elaborate work in another medium, an exercise in visual expression, or a finished work. Pencil marks vary according to the hardness of the graphite lead in the shaft. The harder the lead the finer the line. Pencils are a dry medium, in contrast to pens which apply liquids.
