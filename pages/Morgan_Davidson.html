About

     Morgan Davidson is an illustrator with her BFA in illustration from Ringling College of Art and Desi... She grew up in Southwest Florida in the United States and has always had a strong passion for drawing and an artistic career from a young age.Through her years at Ringling she began to take her interest in nature and fashion and enthusiasm for portraiture and formed a body of work portrayed through vibrant color and rich detail. Though most of her work is drawn traditionally using a wide range of mediums she also enjoys using digital mediums for editing, painting and graphic design. She aspires to build a lifelong career as a freelance artist, helping companies and individuals make their creative ideas come to life.
